Japan is a very interesting and versatile country. Hello, my name is L.C. (a pseudonym), and I welcome you to my page dedicated, as you might guess, to Japan.

The purpose of this site is to show people a little bit of Japan. I have formed a firm opinion that our compatriots' idea of Japan is very far from reality. Some of my friends, hearing the word "Japan", immediately scream: "Oh, Pokemon!". Others do not like Japan, based on the Russian-Japanese past or the tragically famous Russia-Japan football match. Well, you know what they say about opinions, right?

Japan is very colorful country. On the one hand, these are the most modern technologies, and on the other, these are ancient traditions, martial arts and the world famous samurais. On this page I will try to show you the "Land of the Rising Sun" a little.
