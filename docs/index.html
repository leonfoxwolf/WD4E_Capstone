The land of the rising sun

Welcome to the site dedicated to the history and culture of Japan! You can dive directly to its content via menu below. But before, please a quick foreword of its developer.

Hi! I am Konstantin Nikulin. I am a long-time fan of Japan. Ever since the school I was fond of this eastern culture. I even created a site dedicated to Japan as a junior high school examination work for my computer class back at 2003. Now I wanted to recreate it with all my knowledge I gained during my “Web design for Everyone” course at Coursera. You can compare this version of the site to its initial state using links in the footer.
Japan is a marvelous multi-faceted country. Despite the site intended to be as a technical demo, I hope you can find a lot of interesting information about “The land of the rising sun”.
