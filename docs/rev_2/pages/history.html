History of Japan

How can you create a website about any country without telling about the history of this country? Naturally, the whole history of Japan will not fit here, so here is "History of Japan - mega condensed version". I have divided the whole history of "Land of the Rising Sun" into four parts, each of which has its own sub-points. I hope I didn’t “write” anything superfluous into the story and put it all right.
