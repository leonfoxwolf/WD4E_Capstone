Hello dear visitor of this site! I am glad to welcome you to my humble page dedicated, as you can see from the name, to Japan. Here I tried to collect various information about this far from us, but not from our borders, the country of the East, about its way of life, history, traditions and assets. Unfortunately, the site is partially under construction, therefore there is only a small part of the maximum that is ideally planned. Japan is a very interesting and multifaceted state: on the one hand, these are the most modern microtechnologies, and on the other, these are ancient traditions, martial arts and, all over the world, famous samurai.
